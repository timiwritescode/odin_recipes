Papa Odin's favorite fruit punch

Papa odin as we all know, loves to sit at the balcony of Valhalla in Asgard to enjoy the sunset
But did you know that he doesn't do that without his favorite evening drink? Well, now you know

This is the secret to papa odin's awesome experience at the afterglow cinema (get it?)

Ingredients

  • 5cups of water
  • 1 packed cup of sorrel i.e. zobo leaves
  • Â½ tsp vanilla(optional)
  • 12 cloves
  • 1cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 1 kiwi(optional)
  • 2 inches of fresh ginger root(washed)
  • 2cups of pineapple juice(or the peel of one pineapple and it's chunks)
  • the peel of 2 large oranges and their juices
  • the juice of half of a lemon
  • sugar or sweeteners to taste
  • wine or any liquor(optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 5 cups of water
  2. Wash the leaves in a sieve and transfer into the pot of boiling water
  3. Drop in the orange peels and ginger and boil on medium heat for another 15 minutes
  4. Drain and let it cool.
  5. Once cool, add the lemon juice, vanilla, orange juice, drop in the kiwi and stir
  6. Add any sweetener or sugar to taste
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
    (But you'll be missing out on the experience if you do room temperature)
